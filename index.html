About Arpith Ashok

Im Arpith Ashok I am a 22-year-old simple, disciplined, happy, and blessed by the Creator to perceive this beautiful world in my own unique way. With time, I have understood that my Autism limits my ability to express verbally or reciprocate in what the world calls the “Normal” way. But this also means I don’t think of the world around me as much as others, and this is helping me find my own voice, through colours and art. Art is my medium of expression, born out of my struggle. I have difficulty understanding boundaries while writing.
